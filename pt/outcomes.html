The outcomes of this project are a set of 18 recommendations developed with the support of global experts. These recommendations are designed for government officials to aid them as they address the COVID-19 pandemic. Each recommendation is supported by a set of strategies to achieve that recommendation. Strategies in turn detail actions and global examples that guide governments of the specific steps needed for implementing the strategy.
